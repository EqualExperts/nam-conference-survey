# Product Overview

## Product Name
NAM Conference Survey

## Vision Statement
Survey attendees of the North America Equal Experts Conference to collect qualitative and quantitative feedback about their conference experience, making responses easily accessible for Equal Experts leadership.

## Problem Statement

### What problem are we solving?
Gathering relevant feedback about the conference quickly, in a private environment with a customized experience.

### Who experiences this problem?
- **Conference Attendees**: EE employees and associates who attended the North America Equal Experts Conference
- **Conference Organizers**: EE leadership who need to collect, analyze, and report on conference feedback

[TBD - can be explored during discovery]

## Solution Overview
A survey application that allows conference attendees to provide feedback about various aspects of their conference experience. The application:
- Collects both qualitative and quantitative responses
- Provides an easy-to-use interface for attendees
- Makes data easily accessible for EE leadership to review, analyze, and report

## Target Users
1. **Primary**: Conference attendees (EE employees and associates) who need to provide feedback
2. **Secondary**: EE leadership who need to review responses and generate reports

Key areas to measure:
- Response rates
- Time to complete survey
- Quality/completeness of feedback
- Ease of data analysis for leadership

## Constraints & Assumptions

### Technical Constraints
- Must work across multiple devices (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- Simple and easy-to-use interface (technical proficiency should be irrelevant)
- Private and secure environment for responses

### Business Constraints
- MVP stage - focused on core functionality first
- Time-sensitive: attendees should respond while experience is fresh

### Key Assumptions
- Attendees are motivated to provide feedback to improve future conferences
- Leadership needs both qualitative and quantitative data for analysis
- Simple, accessible interface is more important than advanced features
