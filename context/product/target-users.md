# Target Users & Personas

## Primary Persona: Conference Attendee

### Role
EE employees and associates who attended the North America Equal Experts Conference

### Demographics
- **Organization**: Equal Experts (employees and associates)
- **Experience Level**: Generally very experienced consultants
- **Roles**: Software engineers, business analysts, product managers, and other consulting roles
- **Technical Proficiency**: Very advanced in most cases, but technical proficiency should be irrelevant for this application

### Goals
1. Easily provide feedback to conference organizers
2. Improve their experience for future conferences
3. Share thoughts while the conference experience is still fresh in their mind

### Pain Points / Challenges
1. **Device Flexibility**: Need to answer on their chosen device (mobile, tablet, or desktop)
2. **Timely Response**: Want to answer while the experience is still clear in their mind
3. **Simplicity**: Need a simple and easy-to-use interface that doesn't require technical knowledge

### Behaviors & Preferences
- Likely to respond shortly after the conference while memories are fresh
- May use different devices depending on convenience
- Value their time - prefer concise, well-designed surveys
- Motivated to help improve future conferences

### Context of Use
- Completing survey shortly after conference ends
- May be on mobile device while traveling home
- May be at home or office on desktop
- Want to complete quickly but provide thoughtful feedback

### Technical Proficiency
- **Level**: Advanced (experienced consultants and software professionals)
- **Comfort with new tech**: High
- **Important Note**: While technically advanced, the interface should be simple enough that technical proficiency is irrelevant

---

## Secondary Persona: EE Leadership / Conference Organizer

### Role
Equal Experts leadership responsible for conference planning and improvement

### Demographics
- **Organization**: Equal Experts leadership
- **Dual Role**: Also conference attendees who will complete the survey
- **Responsibility**: Conference planning, analysis, and improvement initiatives

### Goals
1. Collect comprehensive feedback from all attendees
2. Easily access and analyze response data
3. Generate reports for stakeholders
4. Identify areas for improvement in future conferences

### Pain Points / Challenges
1. Need data in format that's easy to analyze
2. Want both quantitative metrics and qualitative insights
3. Need to track response rates and completion
4. Time pressure to analyze and report findings

### Additional Context
- This persona is also a conference attendee (dual role)
- Will both complete the survey AND review responses
- Needs dashboard or reporting view in addition to survey interface

### Technical Proficiency
- **Level**: Advanced (experienced consultants and leaders)
- **Comfort with new tech**: High
- **Data Analysis**: Comfortable with data analysis and reporting tools

---

## User Journey Touchpoints

### For Attendees:
1. Receive survey invitation
2. Access survey on preferred device
3. Complete survey questions
4. Submit responses
5. Receive confirmation

### For Leadership:
1. Launch survey to attendees
2. Monitor response rates
3. Review incoming responses
4. Analyze data (qualitative and quantitative)
5. Generate reports
6. Share findings with stakeholders

---

## Notes

- Primary focus for MVP is on the **Conference Attendee** experience
- Leadership/reporting features may be prioritized in later iterations
- Both personas value simplicity and ease of use
- Privacy and confidentiality of responses may be important consideration
